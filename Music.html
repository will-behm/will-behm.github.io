Music C.V.

I started my bluegrass band, Otter Creek, almost four years ago as a senior at Middlebury College, and with the help of my bandmates have grown it into an act that regularly performs around New England. I've played guitar since I was 12, and am obsessed with developing a nuanced, yet authentic sound that is grounded in good technique, strong theory chops, and tradition. Moreover, I absolutely love getting to know folks that care about traditional music - the best part about playing music is connecting with other people!

Here's a link to the Otter Creek Instagram and Website, as well as my personal music instagram. I love teaching, and give acoustic guitar lessons in person or over zoom. Don't hestitate to get in contact if you're interested!
